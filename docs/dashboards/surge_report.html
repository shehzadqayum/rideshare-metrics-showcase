Surge intelligence report

The full hour-by-hour forecast Sentinel generates from six live data sources — reformatted for readability from the pipeline's own output.

How it works. Each hour of the day is scored from five weighted signals — airport arrivals, football egress, ticketed events, transport disruption and weather — combined into a composite surge score, then modified for the day of week. The strongest, most confident hours become the priority windows below, each with a recommended position to be in and by when.
