Earnings & portal automation

The bookkeeping layer: a Node.js tool that keeps a complete, auditable local record of every trip and payment.

Why it exists. Uber shows drivers their history through a web portal — fine for glancing, useless for analysis. This tool drives a real browser session over the portal, extracts every activity and weekly statement, and stores them as structured JSON. A reconciliation step then audits the two against each other (trips seen on screen vs. money actually paid), catching gaps and adjustments. Everything downstream — the metrics engine, the dashboards — trusts this layer for the “what was actually earned” side of the equation. Uber weeks run Monday 04:00 to Monday 04:00, which is why the charts are keyed that way.
